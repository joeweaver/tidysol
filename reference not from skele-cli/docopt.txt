TidySOL, a CLI for tidying up exported COMSOL data.

Usage:
  tidysol.py times <name>
  tidysol.py vars <name>
  tidysol.py headers <name>
  tidysol.py tidy <name> [--time=<time> ... ] [--col=<variable> ...] -o=<outfile>
  tidysol.py -h | --help
  tidysol.py --version

Commands:


Options:
  -h --help     Show this screen.
  -d --describe Include the variable's description.
  --time=<time> ... Create a tidy csv for time steps given
